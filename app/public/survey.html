About You

On a scale of 1-5, please rate your answers to the following questions:

1. If a cat falls asleep in your lap, how likely are you to get up?

2. You are on a date that seems to be going well. Then they mention that they hate cats and could never live with one. How likely are you to go on another date?

3. A friend asks if you could foster a litter of kittens. How likely are you to say yes?

4. A store comes out with a product you already own and you technically don't need another, but this one has a cat print on it. How likely are you to buy it?

5. You are visiting a friend who owns a cat. How long would you wait before going to greet it?

6. How much time would you say you spend looking at cat videos?

7. You hear a cat cafe opened up the next town over. How likely are you to go?

8. If someone was looking at pictures on your phone, how many would be pictures of cats?

9. If you had to choose between tickets to your favorite band's concert or playing with a room full of kittens, how likely would it be that you would choose to play with kittens?

10. And finally, how likely is it that your friends/family will refer to you as the crazy cat lady/man/person?
